Privacy Policy

Commitment to Privacy: Bible Daily is built with a "Privacy-First" philosophy. We believe your spiritual journey is personal. As such, we do not track, collect, or monetize your activity.

1. No Data Collection

We do not collect any Personally Identifiable Information (PII). This means we do not know your name, email address, location, or browsing history. The extension operates entirely on your local machine.

2. Use of Local Storage

To provide a personalized experience, the extension utilizes the chrome.storage API. This data is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to our servers or any third party. We store:

  • Favorite Verses: So you can re-read scriptures that move you.
  • Last Viewed Index: To ensure you see a fresh verse each time you open the app.
  • User Settings: Such as Dark Mode preferences and notification timing.

3. Permissions Disclosure

In accordance with the Chrome Web Store "Single Purpose" policy, we only request permissions necessary for core functionality:

  • Storage: Required to save your "Favorite" verses and app settings locally.
  • Notifications: Required to deliver your daily inspirational verse to your desktop.
  • Alarms: Required to wake the background service worker to trigger scheduled notifications at your preferred time.

4. Third-Party Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your information to outside parties. This extension does not use Google Analytics, tracking pixels, or any form of advertising.
